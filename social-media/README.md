### [Postiz](https://github.com/gitroomhq/postiz-app) ⭐ 28.1k

**Self-hosted Buffer alternative — schedule posts across 15+ social platforms with AI assistance.**

Postiz is a full-stack social media scheduling platform you run on your own server. Connect Instagram, X/Twitter, TikTok, LinkedIn, Discord, Facebook, YouTube, Pinterest, Reddit, and more from a single dashboard. Schedule posts, analyse performance metrics, collaborate with team members via role-based access, and let AI help optimise content before publishing. Workflow orchestration via Temporal handles complex multi-step scheduling reliably. Available as a self-hosted Docker deployment or managed cloud.

**Stack:** TypeScript, Next.js, NestJS, Prisma, Temporal
**License:** AGPL-3.0

**Why it's notable:** Buffer, Hootsuite, and every other social media scheduler are SaaS-first — you share credentials with their servers and pay monthly. Postiz is the serious self-hosted alternative: your OAuth tokens stay on your infrastructure, your scheduling data stays in your database, and you're not locked into someone else's pricing. 28.1k stars and 5k forks on a category that seemed permanently owned by closed-source SaaS signals real developer appetite for an independent option. Temporal for workflow orchestration is an architectural choice that takes reliability seriously — missed schedules and partial failures are handled, not silently dropped.
