### [SearXNG](https://github.com/searxng/searxng) ⭐ 26.9k

**The definitive self-hosted private metasearch engine.**

Aggregates results from dozens of search services — Google, Bing, DuckDuckGo, and more — without passing your identity to any of them. No tracking, no profiling, no search history stored. Self-host it for yourself or your team, configure which engines to query, and get clean aggregated results with none of the surveillance. 277 contributors and 9,000+ commits deep.

**Stack:** Python · HTML · TypeScript
**License:** AGPL-3.0
**Deployment:** Self-hosted (Docker supported)

**Why it's notable:** SearXNG is the fork of Searx that became the actively maintained standard — it's what you find running behind most privacy-respecting search frontends. The concept is simple and the execution is rock solid: one URL, all the search engines, none of the tracking. When you want to stop feeding your queries to Google but don't want to compromise on results, this is the answer.
