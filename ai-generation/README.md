### [ComfyUI](https://github.com/Comfy-Org/ComfyUI) ⭐ 108k

**The most powerful node-based visual engine for AI generation — images, video, audio, 3D.**

ComfyUI gives you a graph-based canvas where you wire together model loaders, samplers, conditioning nodes, upscalers, and postprocessors into fully custom AI generation pipelines — all without writing code. Supports virtually every major diffusion model: Stable Diffusion 1.x/2.x/XL/3, Flux, SDXL, Mochi, HunyuanVideo, Stable Audio, and more. Runs entirely offline on your own GPU (NVIDIA, AMD, Intel, Apple Silicon) with smart memory management that fits large models into limited VRAM.

**Stack:** Python, CUDA, cross-platform (Windows, Linux, macOS)
**License:** GPL-3.0

**Why it's notable:** When we evaluated AUTOMATIC1111 (the previous standard for local AI image generation), we noted it was in maintenance mode and ComfyUI had surpassed it — 108k stars confirms that judgment. The node-based approach unlocks workflows that menu-driven tools can't: chain ControlNet + LoRA + upscaler + inpainting in a single graph, save it, share it, version it. The ecosystem of custom nodes extends into video, audio, and 3D generation. The most capable local AI generation environment that exists, period.
