### [Home Assistant](https://github.com/home-assistant/core) ⭐ 85.7k

**The definitive self-hosted smart home platform.**

Open-source home automation that puts local control and privacy first. Connect and control thousands of smart home devices — lights, thermostats, locks, sensors, cameras, energy meters — through a single unified interface. Define automations, schedules, and scenes in YAML or via the visual automation editor. Everything runs on your own hardware (Raspberry Pi, a NUC, a VM) with zero data sent to any cloud by default.

**Stack:** Python, asyncio, MQTT
**License:** Apache 2.0

**Why it's notable:** Home Assistant is the undisputed standard for self-hosted smart home control — 85.7k stars, 4,672 contributors, 1,500+ releases, and a monthly release cadence that hasn't slipped in years. It supports 3,000+ integrations covering virtually every smart home ecosystem: Zigbee, Z-Wave, Matter, Thread, Philips Hue, Google Home, Apple HomeKit, IKEA Tradfri, and hundreds more. The philosophy — local control, privacy-first, no vendor lock-in — is exactly what makes it the right answer for anyone who wants their home to be smart without handing control to a third party. The community size means almost every device and edge case is already solved somewhere.
