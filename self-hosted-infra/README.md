### [Coolify](https://github.com/coollabsio/coolify) ⭐ 52.6k

**Self-host everything — your open-source alternative to Heroku, Netlify, and Vercel.**

Coolify turns any VPS, bare metal server, or Raspberry Pi into your own cloud platform. Connect a server via SSH, then deploy applications, databases, and 280+ one-click services (WordPress, Plausible, Supabase, Ghost, and hundreds more) through a clean web UI. Handles SSL certificates, reverse proxying, environment variables, deployments from Git, and automatic backups — everything a hosted platform would give you, running on infrastructure you own.

**Stack:** PHP (Laravel), Docker, Alpine.js
**License:** Apache 2.0

**Why it's notable:** Heroku's free tier died, Netlify and Vercel have limits and price cliffs, Render and Railway are convenient until they're not. Coolify is the answer to "I want the convenience of a PaaS but I want to own the server." 14,000+ commits, 280+ one-click service templates, and 52.6k stars signal a mature platform rather than a weekend project. Apache 2.0 licence, no usage-based pricing, no vendor lock-in — just your server running your apps.
