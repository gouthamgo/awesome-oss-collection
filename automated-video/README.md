### [RedditVideoMakerBot](https://github.com/elebumm/RedditVideoMakerBot) ⭐ 8.7k

**Turn any Reddit thread into a ready-to-upload short-form video with one command.**

Give it a subreddit or thread URL and it handles the entire pipeline: fetches the post and top comments, generates TTS narration for each line, overlays the audio onto background footage (Minecraft, GTA, or your own clips), and exports a finished video ready for TikTok, YouTube Shorts, or Instagram Reels — no manual editing at any step. Filters NSFW content automatically, prevents duplicate videos, and supports light/dark mode Reddit screenshots.

**Stack:** Python, HTML, Shell
**License:** GPL-3.0

**Why it's notable:** This is the tool that spawned the entire "Reddit content on TikTok" genre — the format is everywhere, and this bot is what most people used to build it. 8.7k stars and 2.1k forks for a single-purpose automation script is remarkable signal. It does one thing and does it completely: zero editing required between "run command" and "upload video." For content creators who want to automate a Reddit clip channel, this is the only serious open-source option. Actively maintained with releases through 2025 and 16 open issues — healthy project posture.
